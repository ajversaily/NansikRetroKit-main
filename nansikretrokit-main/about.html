About Nansik Retro Kits

Nansik Retro Kit was founded in 2023 by Prakrit Biswa. Based in Columbus Ohio, Nansik Retro Kits is a local jersey vendor for everyone that dreams! Growing up we all dream of becoming a soccer player and having our favorite player's name on our back! We imagine ourselves as our idols inspired by their style, skills, and their passion for the beautiful game. We create memories with this beautiful sport and those memories live on with us our whole life. This is the one place where we forget our problems and express ourselves fully. So today, let Nansik Retro Kits help you express yourself today! Be the player you want to be and see yourself as who you want to be one day. It's not just a jersey. It's who we are!
